2005 Mesache Somontano
Pirineos

España

A blend of 50% Garnacha, 30% Cabernet Sauvignon, 10% Syrah and 10% Parraleta. Parraleta is unique to the Somontano region.

Price: $11
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - This wine is black and opaque as the night. With a muted bouquet this wine draws the drinker into a dry, flavorful journey ending in a curious, unusual aftertaste.
