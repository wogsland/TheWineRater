Crusted Port
Churchill's

Portugal

Non-vintage Oporto Aged in Oak Casks and Bottled in 1988.

20.0% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - Light and tawny in color, this port is quite aged. There is a strong agedness in the bouquet and the flavor is aged and spicy with a sweet, long aftertaste that has strong oak and a numbing finish.
