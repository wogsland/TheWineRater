2008 Steen Chenin Blanc Western Cape South Africa
Sebeka

Malmesbury, South Africa

Bottled with a plastic cork by Swartland Winery.

Price: $9
12.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - This plastic-corked Chenin Blanc has a shining yellow hue to it. The bouquet is grassy and light, while the flavor is tart and interesting. The long, tart, dry aftertaste demands the accompaniment of a good fromage.

2009 tasting - A light yellow wine, this Chenin Blanc has a lovely, grassy bouquet and a short, tart flavor.
