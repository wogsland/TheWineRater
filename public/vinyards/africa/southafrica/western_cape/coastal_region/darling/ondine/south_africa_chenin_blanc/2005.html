2005 South Africa Chenin Blanc
Ondine

South Africa

Wine of Origin Darling

Price: $12
13% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A nice yellow wine, this Chenin Blanc has a strong apple bouquet coupled to a tart, apply flavor and a dry apply finish that is long lasting and eventually veers toward grass. The strength of the apple in the wine mellowed after being open for an hour or so though.
