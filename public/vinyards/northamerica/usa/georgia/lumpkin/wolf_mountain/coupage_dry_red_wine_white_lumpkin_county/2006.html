2006 Coupage Dry Red Wine White / Lumpkin County
Wolf Mountain Vineyards

Dahlonega, GA

65% Merlot and 35% Cabernet Sauvignon grown at 1800 ft and aged in French oak before blending.

Price: $23
13.1% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - A blackish red, this blend has a wild, eastern bouquet with a dry, fair flavor and a short, sweet, moist aftertaste. Quite possibly I also caught the hint of added sugar. It's hard to tell if this wine will soften in the bottle or deteriorate with age.
