Fennville

Michigan

The Fennville AVA in Michigan is home to vineyards of the state's winegrowing industry. It was declared on Sept 18, 1981 and includes parts of Allegan and Van Buren counties. In 1983 the larger Lake Michigan Shore was created around it.
