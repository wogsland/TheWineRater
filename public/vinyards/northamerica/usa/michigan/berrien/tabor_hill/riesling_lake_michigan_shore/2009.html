2009 Riesling Lake Michigan Shore
Tabor Hill

Buchanan, MI


Price: $9
12.0% alcohol
750 ml

2010 tasting - A very light, almost silver wine, this Riesling has an excellent, sweet bouquet with hints of orange and pears and followed up by a vivacious, grapy bouquet. The aftertaste is tart at first, but develops into a mellowing sweet. Good chilled.
