NV Vinscatto American
Stone Hill Winery

Hermann, Missouri

When most of the 2007 Vignoles vintage was destroyed by cold weather Stone Hill sourced Muscat Canelli and Riesling from out of state to blend with what remained.

Price: $11
12.0% alcohol
750 ml

2010 tasting - A yellow blend with a sweet bouquet, this wine has a sweet, complex flavor with apple and pear hints coupled to a very grapy finish.

2009 tasting - Nearly white in color, this blend has a very sweet bouquet coupled to a smooth, sweet flavor and a nice, sweet finish.
