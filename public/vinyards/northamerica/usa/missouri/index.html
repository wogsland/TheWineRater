Missouri

Missouri is home the the oldest American Viticultural Area (AVA) in the US, and has a long history of wine production. Missouri Norton was world reknowned as one of the best wines in the world before Prohibition. The past few decades has seen a renaissance in Missouri.

Viticultural Regions:

Augusta
Boone County
Franklin County
Gasconade County
Hermann
Ozark Highlands
Ozark Mountain
Phelps County
