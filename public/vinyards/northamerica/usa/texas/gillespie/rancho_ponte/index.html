Rancho Ponte Vineyard

Fredricksburg, TX

www.ranchoponte.com

A husband and wife team (he makes the wine, she runs the tasting room), Rancho Ponte puts no AVAs on their bottles but source their grapes from around Brownsville.

$10 tasting fee includes souvenir glass and 6 tastings.

Vintages:

Sauvignon Blanc 2010
Meritage 2008
Rancher's Reserve 2009
Triple M Blend 2008
Messis Port
Renegade Vintage Port 2003
