2004 Claret les trois dame Texas
Becker Vineyards

Stonewall, TX

A Bordeaux style red wine blend of Cabernet Sauvignon 40%, Merlot 37%, Malbec 10%, Cabernet Franc 10% and Petit Verdot 3%.

Price: $20
13.5% alcohol
750ml

2006 tasting - This thickly red Claret has a very floral excellent bouquet and a good, floral, light taste.

2007 tasting - This deep, dark red wine has an intensely floral bouquet coupled to a complex flavor with hints of chocolate, flowers and a long aftertaste.
