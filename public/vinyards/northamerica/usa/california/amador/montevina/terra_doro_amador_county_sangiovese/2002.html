2002 Terra d'Oro Amador County Sangiovese
MonteVina

Plymouth, CA


Price: $15
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This clear red has an oaky, sweet smell coupled to a light, earthy flavor with hints of sweet and a dry, tart, earthy aftertaste.

2008 tasting - A light, transparent red, this Sangiovese has a strong, jammy, tart bouquet coupled to a tart, light flavor and a California Mountain finish. Requires a bit of time to oxidize and bring out all the flavors.

2010 tasting - This wine is an orange-brown red color with an oaky bouquet. The spicy, aged flavor is an excellent expression of the Sangiovese grape and the aftertaste is surprisingly moist for the alcohol content.
