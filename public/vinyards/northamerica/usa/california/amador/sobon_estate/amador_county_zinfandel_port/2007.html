2007 Amador County Zinfandel Port
Sobon Estate

California

Certified Organic by CCOF.

Price: $18
18.5% alcohol
375 ml

2010 tasting - An opaque black wine, this port has a dry, sweet bouquet developing into a spicy, chewy flavor and a spicy finish.

2009 tasting - This dark, slightly purple red port has nice, fat legs. The bouquet is sweet and light, providing an entrepôt to the chewy flavor with sweet hints and an aftertaste that bites before drying out.
