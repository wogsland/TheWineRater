2007 Amador County Zinfandel Cougar Hill
Sobon Estate

Plymouth, CA

Vineyard planted in 1928 and named for cougar tracks often found there. Made with CCOF certified organic grapes.

Price: $18
Alcohol: 15.1%
750 ml

2010 tasting - A transparent red with a fruity bouquet hinting of butter, this Zinfandel has a dry, chewy flavor which hints of the mountain terroir and develops into an extremely dry aftertaste.

2009 tasting - This blackish red Zin has a sweetish bouquet accompanied by a smooth, dry flavor and a tart, chewy aftertaste.
