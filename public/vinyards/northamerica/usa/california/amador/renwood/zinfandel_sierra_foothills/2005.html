2005 Zinfandel Sierra Foothills
Renwood

Plymouth, CA

13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting ($10) - This Zinfandel is like a cool summer draught from the mountains. Dark red in in color, this wine has an oaky bouquet coupled to a fruity, very light flavor and a tart, moist aftertaste.

2009 tasting ($12) - This dark transparent red has a sweetish, oaky bouquet. The flavor is sweet, but tannic for a Zin and the aftertaste exhibits mineral hints.
