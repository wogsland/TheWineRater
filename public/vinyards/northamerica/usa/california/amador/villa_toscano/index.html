Villa Toscano

Plymouth, CA

www.villatoscano.com

No Tasting Fee.

The sister winery of Bella Piazza, Villa Toscano makes wines exclusively from Shenandoah Valley grapes. The two wineries share a winemaker and barrel tastings are offered daily for the more popular wines which are sold as futures. There is also a bistro onsite.
