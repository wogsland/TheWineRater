2004 California Shenandoah Valley Port
Fox Creek Vineyard
Villa Toscano

Plymouth, CA

Made with Zinfandel from the Fox Creek Vineyard.

Price: $32
20.0% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - An opaque red with a sweet mountain bouquet evoking the terroir of the Shenandoah Valley. The balanced flavor is perfection in a glass. The dry, long aftertaste in reminiscent of mountain soil and the American River.

2010 tasting - This dark red port has a sweet, light bouquet coupled to a chewy, sweet flavor with a strong California mountain terroir and a chewy, dry finish.

2009 tasting - This blackish red port has a sweetish bouquet coupled to a sweet flavor with a good mountain terroir. The aftertaste is dry, tart and chewy.
