2007 Amador County Kikas
Dobra Zemlja

Plymouth, CA

A fortified, port-style wine produced from Zinfandel bottled unfiltered and unfined.

Price: $26
20.0% alcohol
750 ml

2010 tasting - A dark, transparent red, this wine has a sweet, interesting bouquet coupled to a spicy, slightly frizzante flavor. It has not aged well. The aftertaste is dry and chewy.

2009 tasting - A light red with a spicy bouquet, this port-style wine has a light, spicy flavor that is only slightly sweet. The aftertaste, however, is sweet, short and smooth.
