2005 Three Ranches Cabernet Sauvignon North Coast
Rock & Vine

Oakville, CA

Actually a blend of 79% Solano County Cabernet Sauvignon, 13% Napa County Merlot, 5% Sonoma County Cabernet Franc, 2% Napa County Petite Verdot and 1% Napa County Malbec.

14.1% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - An opaque red, this Cab has a dry, oaky bouquet coupled to a juicy flavor and a dry, slightly chewy finish.
