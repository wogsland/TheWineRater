2003 Aria Napa Valley White Port
Prager Winery & Port Works

St. Helena, California

Bottled in 2007 and named for eldest granddaughter of Peter Prager, this white port is made from Chardonnay.

Price: $48.50
18% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This clear golden hue wine has a bouquet with nuttiness hinting of almonds coupled to a sweet flavor and a very nutty, dry aftertaste which reminds one of walnuts or pecans.
