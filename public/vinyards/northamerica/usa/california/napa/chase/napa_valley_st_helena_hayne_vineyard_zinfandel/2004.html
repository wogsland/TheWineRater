2004 Napa Valley St Helena Hayne Vineyard Zinfandel
Chase

St Helena, CA

The Hayne Vineyard was planted on St. George rootstock in 1903. This vintage was aged 20 months in French oak.

Price: $30
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A dark transparent red, this Zinfandel has a jammy, sweet bouquet coupled to a full flavor which is spicy and chewy. The aftertaste is extremely dry.
