2002 Alexander Valley Cabernet Sauvignon
Silver Oak

Oakville, California

This 100% Cabernet wine was harvested in four weeks during September and October. Aged in 50% new / 50% old American oak for 25 months before spending 15 months in the bottle prior to release.

Price: $60
750 ml (also available in 1.5L, 3L and 6L sizes)
13.6% alcohol
50,000 cases produced

2007 tasting - This dark red Cabernet has a good but young bouquet and a good flavor which is slightly floral but quite dry, lasting into a long, complex aftertaste. This is a wine with development potential.
