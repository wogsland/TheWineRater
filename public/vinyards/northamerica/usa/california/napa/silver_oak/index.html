Silver Oak

Oakville, California

www.silveroak.com

Silver Oak is one of the super-premium wineries in Napa Valley with vineyards there and in the nearby Alexander Valley. They only make Cabernet and it can be some of the best in the world.

Vintages:

Napa Valley Cabernet Sauvignon 1998 2002 2003
Alexander Valley Cabernet Sauvignon 2002
