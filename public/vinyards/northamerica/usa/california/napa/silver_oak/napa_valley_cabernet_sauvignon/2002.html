2002 Napa Valley Cabernet Sauvignon
Silver Oak

Oakville, California

Actually a blend of 83% Cabernet Sauvignon, 11% Merlot, 4% Cabernet Franc and 2% Petit Verdot harvested in four weeks during September and October. Aged in 100% new American oak for 25 months before spending 20 months in the bottle prior to release.

Price: $100
750 ml (also available in 1.5L, 3L and 6L sizes)
14.2% alcohol
25,000 cases produced

2007 tasting - This is a very dark red wine with an excellent interesting bouquet which hints of berry and spice. The flavor is very dry with hints of berry and sandy earth and continues into a dry aftertaste.
