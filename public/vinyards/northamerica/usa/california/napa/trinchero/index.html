Trinchero Winery

St. Helena, CA

www.trincherowinery.com

Trinchero makes the Trinchero Family series of California wines as well as single vineyard bottlings of a diverse set of varietals. The wines are named in honor of Mario Trinchero, who bought Sutter Home in the 1940's and built it into a national brand.

Family Vintages:

Sauvignon Blanc Santa Barbara County 2007
Cabernet Sauvignon California 2004 2005

Single Vineyard Vintages:

St. Helena Napa Valley Estate Bottled Cabernet Franc 2004
Lewelling Vineyard Cabernet Sauvignon 2002
Vista Montone Chardonnay 2005
Petit Verdot 2004
Mary's Vineyard Sauvignon Blanc 2004
