2003 Napa Valley Cabernet Sauvignon
Franciscan Oakville Estate

Rutherford, CA

89% Cabernet Sauvignon, 10% Merlot, 1% Petit Verdot aged 18 months in French and American oak.

Price: $29
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2006 tasting - This completely opaque red wine has a thick, slightly floral bouquet and a dry flavor with dries further and lasts long after.

2007 tasting - This dark red Cabernet has very oaky, spicy bouquet and a complex, excellent flavor with an interesting oaky aftertaste.
