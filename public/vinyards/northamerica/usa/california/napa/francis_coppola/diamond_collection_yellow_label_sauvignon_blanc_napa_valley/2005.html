2005 Diamond Collection Yellow Label
Sauvignon Blanc Napa Valley
Francis Coppola

Geyserville, CA


Price: $14
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A nice, yellow wine, this Sauvignon Blanc has a strong, oaky smell that hearkens a barrelroom. The flavor which follows is also oaky with citrusy hints and a young, dry finish. Probably needs a few more years in the bottle, but excellent even at this age.
