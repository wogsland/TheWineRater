1990 Sonoma County Zinfandel
Marietta Cellars

Geyserville, CA

Grapes mostly form dry-farmed vineyards of the Alexander and Dry Creek Valleys.

Price: $12
13.7% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - An old enough bottle that the cork disintegrated during opening, this opaque, light red Zinfandel greets the drinker with a bouquet like an old winery. The flavor which follows is old and interesting with a tart, extremely dry bouquet.
