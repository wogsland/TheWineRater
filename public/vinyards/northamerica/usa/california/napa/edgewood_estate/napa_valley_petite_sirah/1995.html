1995 Napa Valley Petite Sirah
Edgewood Estate

St Helena, CA


13.0% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - This Petite Sirah is opaque with a purple tinge. The bouquet is complex, aged with slight berry hints and the flavor is dry, mellow and lasts seemingly forever on the palate. There are butter and prune hints as the wine opens up in the mouth. Flawless.
