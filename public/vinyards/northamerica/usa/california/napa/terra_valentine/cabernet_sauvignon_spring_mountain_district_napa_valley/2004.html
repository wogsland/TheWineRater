2004 Cabernet Sauvignon Spring Mountain District Napa Valley

St Helena, CA


Price: $37

2009 tasting - This blackish red wine has a dry, oaky bouquet. Rough and tannic for a Cab, the flavor is unbalanced and somewhat overwhelmed by the high alcohol content. The finish is tart and strong. Methinks it needs age to soften though.
