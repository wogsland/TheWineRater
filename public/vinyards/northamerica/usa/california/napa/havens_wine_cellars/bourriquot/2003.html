2003 Napa Valley Red Wine Bourriquot
Havens

Napa, CA

A blend of 33% Merlot and 67% Cabernet Franc.

14.5% alcohol
375 ml

2011 tasting - This wine is a brownish red with a fair amount of sediment. The deep bouquet has a strong Napa Valley terroir. Coupled to that is an intense Cab Franc flavor which is quite aged (past its prime). The finish is dry on the tongue and somewhat puckering.

More about Cabernet Franc, Merlot.
