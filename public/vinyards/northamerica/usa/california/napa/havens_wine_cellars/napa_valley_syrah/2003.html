2003 Syrah Napa Valley
Havens

Napa, CA

14.5% alcohol
375 ml

2011 tasting - Dark, opaque and inviting; a Syrah with a bouquet of sun-dried fruit preceding a rounded, balanced flavor with a bit of dry spiciness in the finish

2010 tasting - A dark, opaque red, this Syrah has a deep, fruity bouquet coupled to a tart, complex flavor with a dry finish. Interesting take on the grape.

2008 tasting - This opaque red has a fruity, chewy bouquet coupled to a sharp, tart, slightly chewy flavor and a young, dry finish.
