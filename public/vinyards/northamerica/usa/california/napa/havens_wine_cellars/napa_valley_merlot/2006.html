2006 Merlot Napa Valley
Havens

Napa, CA

Actually a blend of 94% Merlot, 4% Cabernet Franc and 2% Syrah aged 19 months in 60-gallon oak barrels, 25% new (85% French, 15% American) and 75% neutral oak.

Price: $11
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A dark, semi-opaque red with a tight fruit bouquet and a tart, strong flavor coupled to a bold, long finish.

2010 tasting - A semi-opaque red with a strong berry bouquet, this Merlot has an intense, grapy dry flavor with dry, chewy aftertaste.
