2006 Cabernet Sauvignon North Coast
H by Havens

Napa, CA

Actually a blend of 75% Cabernet Sauvignon, 12.5% Merlot and 12.5% Syrah. Aged 20 months in French oak.

Price: $11
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - A dark red, this wine has a strong, Merlot bouquet coupled to a dry, complex flavor and a dry finish.

2010 tasting - A dark red with a fair bouquet, this Cabernet Sauvignon has a fruity, chewy flavor and a dry aftertaste.
