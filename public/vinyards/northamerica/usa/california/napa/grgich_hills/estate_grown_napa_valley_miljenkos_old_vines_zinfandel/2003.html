2003 Estate Grown Napa Valley
Miljenko's Old Vines Zinfandel
Grgich Hills

Rutherford, CA

From vines which are 115 years old. Should last for ten years and get jammier with age.

Price: $69
15.1% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A blackish red, this Zinfandel has an oaky bouquet coupled to an excellent, very dry flavor with mineral notes and a long, well-rounded finish.

2008 tasting - A blackish, purple, this completely opaque Zinfandel has a dry, oaky, slightly jammy bouquet paired to a very dry, jammy, complex flavor and an extremely dry aftertaste.

2007 tasting - This dark red Zin has a heavy, earthy bouquet and a slightly jammy, deep flavor with a long, complex aftertaste. Definiteely worth picking up a bottle.
