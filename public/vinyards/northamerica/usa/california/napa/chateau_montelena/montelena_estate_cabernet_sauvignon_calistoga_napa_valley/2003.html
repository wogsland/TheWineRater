2003 The Montelena Estate Cabernet Sauvignon
Calistoga Napa Valley

Chateau Montelena
California

750 ml

2008 tasting - This Cabernet is dark and blackish red in color. The bouquet is excellent, with an oakiness that continues into the taste, which is dry, exhibiting the solidity Napa can provide to this varietal while having blackberry hints and a very dry, jammy finish.
