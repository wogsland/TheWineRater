2011 tasting - An opaque, purplish red, this Cab has a dry, wooden bouquet followed up by a fruit flavor with strong Napa terroir and a dry finish.

2010 tasting - A dark, semi-opaque wine with a perfectly round, Napa bouquet, this Cabernet Sauvignon has a chewy, sweet flavor coupled to a dry, complex finish that is evocative of the Napa Valley terroir.

2007 tasting - This dark red has a light bouquet and a very dry, light flavor.
