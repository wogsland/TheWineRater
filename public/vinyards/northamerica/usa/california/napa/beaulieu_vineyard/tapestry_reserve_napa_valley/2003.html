2003 Tapestry Reserve Napa Valley
Beaulieu Vineyard

Rutherford, CA

A blend of Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Petite Verdot, Cabernet Franc and Malbec.

Price: $50
14.2% alcohol
750 ml

2010 tasting - A dark, opaque red, this blend has a deep, fruity bouquet coupled to a grapy, complex flavor and a dry, chewy finish.

2008 tasting - This dark red blend has nice legs. The bouquet is incredibly rich with fruity, oaky and dry notes. The flavor which follows is strong, dry and perfectly blended with a superlatively dry finish.

2007 tasting - This dark red has a dry, light bouquet coupled to a good, sweet flavor and a dry aftertaste. Just plain excellent.

More about Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Malbec, Merlot and Petite Verdot.
