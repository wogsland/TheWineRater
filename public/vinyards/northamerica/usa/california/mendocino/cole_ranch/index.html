Cole Ranch

Mendocino County, California

The Cole Ranch AVA is located in the mountains of central Mendocino County. It was declared on 15 April 1983 and at a mere 189 acres remains the smallest appellation in the US. All the vineyards within it (~60 acres) are owned by Esterlina Vineyards.
