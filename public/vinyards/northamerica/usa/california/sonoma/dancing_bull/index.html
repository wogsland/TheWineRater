Dancing Bull

Healdsburg, CA

www.dancingbullwines.com

Owned by Gallo, until the 2006 vintage the Dancing Bull wines were produced under the Rancho Zabaco label, but beginning with that vintage Gallo spun it off to it's own label.

Vintages:

Chardonnay California 2005
Merlot California 2004
Zinfandel California 2001 2004 2005 2006
