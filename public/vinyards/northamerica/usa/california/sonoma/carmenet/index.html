Carmenet Winery

Glen Ellen, CA

A 1996 fire destroyed nearly 70% of Carmenet's Sonoma Valley vineyard grapes, including a large part of its 20-year-old vines. The winery managed to stay in business for a few more years, but in 2001 Carmenet split up into its three different labels: Carmenet, Moon Mountain Vineyards and Dynamite.

Vintages:
