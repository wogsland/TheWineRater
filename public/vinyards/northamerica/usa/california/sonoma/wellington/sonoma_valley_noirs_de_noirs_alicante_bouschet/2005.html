2005 Sonoma Valley Old Vines Noirs de Noirs Alicante Bouschet
Wellington Vineyards

Glen Ellen, CA

84% Alicante Bouschet blended with 12% Lenoir, 3% Grand Noir and 1% Petite Bouschet.

Price: $25
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A completely opaque purple, this wine has a fruity, tart bouquet coupled to a grapy, tart flavor and a very chewy, light aftertaste. Some sediment.

2007 tasting - A completely opaque, purplish red, this Alicante Bouschet has a sweet, interesting bouquet with a light, sweet, complex flavor and a dryaftertaste with hints of sweet.
