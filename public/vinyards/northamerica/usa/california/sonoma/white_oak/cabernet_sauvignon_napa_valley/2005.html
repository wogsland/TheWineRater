2005 Cabernet Sauvignon Napa Valley
White Oak Vineyards & Winery

Healdsburg, CA

90% Cabernet Sauvignon blended with 7% Merlot and 3% Cabernet Franc.

Price: $30
14.4% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - This opaque red wine has an strong bouquet – deep and Cabernet. The flavor which follows is just excellent.

2011 tasting - A blackish, opaque wine, this Cab has a oaky bouquet with nice terroir. The flavor is excellent with currant notes and a dry, long aftertaste.
