2007 Sauvignon Blanc Russian River Valley
White Oak Vineyards & Winery

Healdsburg, CA

100% Sauvignon Blanc grown on alluvial river deposits (well-drained sandy soil with gravel outcroppings) and harvested in the early mornings of September 7-12. Fermented in stainless steel and aged sur lie.
2934 cases produced.

Price: $16
13.6% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - This Sauvignon Blanc is a light transparent yellow. There are grassy notes on the bouquet and fruitiness in the flavor. You can also taste a bit of the sur lie. The aftertaste has slightly fruity hints. Overall quite balanced and still young.
