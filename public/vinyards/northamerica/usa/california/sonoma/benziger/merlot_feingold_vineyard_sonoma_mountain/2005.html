2005 Merlot Feingold Vineyard
Sonoma Mountain
Benziger Family Winery

Glen Ellen, CA

Certified organic by Stellar Certification Services, the grapes where harvested Oct 19, 2005, aged in French oak and the wine bottled March 7, 2007.
Less than 400 cases (16 barrels) made.

14.3% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A dark, semi-opaque red with a deep, soft bouquet, this Merlotr has incredible structure. Chewy, tannic & reminiscent of fir trees.

2009 tasting ($14) - A blackish red with a deep bouquet hinting of currants, this Merlot has a light, dry, slightly sweet flavor coupled to an extremely dry aftertaste.

2008 tasting ($20) - A fairly opaque dark red, this Merlot has an oaky, fruity bouquet. This flavor which follows is dry, light and slightly fruity with a tart, dry aftertaste. One can almost taste the dry California dirt in the glass.
