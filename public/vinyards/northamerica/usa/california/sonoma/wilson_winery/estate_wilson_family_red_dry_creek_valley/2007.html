2007 Estate Wilson Family Red Dry Creek Valley
Wilson Winery

Healdsburg, CA

A blend of 57% Zinfandel, 14% Cabernet Sauvignon, 14% Petite Sirah and 14% Syrah.
330 cases produced.

15.5% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A dark red with an oaky bouquet, this red wine has a dry, sweet, high alcohol flavor coupled to a very dry finish.

2009 tasting - A dark red with a nice, dry bouquet, this blend has a chewy, slightly sweet flavor with a dry, chewy finish.
