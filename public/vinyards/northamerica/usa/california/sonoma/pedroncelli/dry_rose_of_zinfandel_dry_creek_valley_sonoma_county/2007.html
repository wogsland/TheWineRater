2010 tasting - A light, clear Rosé with a muted bouquet, this Zinfandel possesses a tart, interesting flavor with a sweet, grapy finish. The alcohol is a little high for a Rosé though.

2009 tasting - This light, transparent Rosé has a grapy bouquet coupled to a mouth-puckeringly tart flavor and a slightly sweet finish. Too much alcohol to be refreshing on a hot day, though.
