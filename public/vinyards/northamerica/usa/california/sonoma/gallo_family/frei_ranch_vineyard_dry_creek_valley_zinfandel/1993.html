1993 Frei Ranch Vineyard Zinfandel Dry Creek Valley
Gallo Sonoma

Healdsburg, CA


Price: $12
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - A dark, translucent red, this Zin has a dry bouquet with excellent terroir. The flavor, too, is a perfect example of the Dry Creek Valley terroir: dry, complex, berry hints, oak hints and an aged finish.

2011 tasting - A light, slightly brownish red, this Zinfandel has a deep, fruity bouquet evolving into a tart flavor that finishes spicily. Well-aged indeed.

2010 tasting - A cloudy, brownish red with an aged, corky bouquet, this Zinfandel has a strong, aged flavor and an extremely dry, aged finish.

2009 tasting - A dark, translucent red, this Zinfandel has a deep fruity bouquet coupled to a light flavor that is slightly chewy and slightly tart. The aftertaste is dry on the palate and there is a strong minerality throughout.
