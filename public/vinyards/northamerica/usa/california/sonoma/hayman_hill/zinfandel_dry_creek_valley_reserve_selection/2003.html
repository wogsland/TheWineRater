2003 Zinfandel Dry Creek Valley Reserve Selection
Hayman & Hill

Gonzales, CA

Selection no. 45 winemakers collection.
Grown on red soil.

14.3% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting ($12) - Dark ruby in color, this Zin has a bouquet which is dry with fruity and chocolaty hints. The flavor light and tart with a dry, chewy finish.

2009 tasting ($13)- A dark, transparent red, this Zinfandel has a dry, fruity bouquet coupled to a complex, old, slightly chewy flavor and a fruity aftertaste with hints of currant.
