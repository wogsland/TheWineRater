Sausal Winery

Healdsburg, California

www.sausalwinery.com

Located on an Alexander Valley estate that once grew a variety of fruits in addition to winegrapes, Sausal has over the past decades converted entirely to wine production.

Vintages:

Family Zinfandel Alexander Valley 2003
Private Reserve Zinfandel Alexander Valley 2004
Cabernet Sauvignon Alexander Valley 2003
Sogni d'Oro Alexander Valley NV
Petite Sirah Alexander Valley 2005
Sangiovese Alexander Valley 2005
