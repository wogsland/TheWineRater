Sonoma Valley

Sonoma County, California

The Sonoma Valley AVA encompasses Sonoma County's Sonoma River Valley where it runs down into the San Pablo Bay. Since its creation it has been subdivided into several subappellations. The Sonoma Valley also overlaps the Los Carneros region.
