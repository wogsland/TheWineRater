2004 Sonoma Valley Cabernet Sauvignon Port
B. R. Cohn Winery

Glen Ellen, CA

100% Cabernet Sauvignon aged 20 months in French oak.

270 cases produced.

Price: $40
19.3% alcohol
375 ml

2007 tasting - This opaque, blackish red wine has a sweet bouquet which hints of oak matched to a nice light taste that is sweet and complex with a long, many flavored finish. Should last 20 years.

2008 tasting - A pitch black red, this port has a strong bouquet of soil and oak that is quintessentially California. The flavor is strong and tart but a bit chemy. Oxidation is needed to soften it.

2009 tasting - A dark, opaque, very long-legged red, this port has a spicy, grapy bouquet and flavor coupled to an extremely dry finish. This wine just gets better with age.
