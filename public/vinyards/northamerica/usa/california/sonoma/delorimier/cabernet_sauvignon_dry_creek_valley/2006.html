2006 Cabernet Sauvignon Dry Creek Valley
deLorimier Winery

Geyserville, CA

Aged 36 months in French oak barrels and bottled unfined. 290 cases produced.

14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - This semi-opaque red has short legs. Its oaky, earthy bouquet is coupled to a light, chewy flavor and a dry, excellent finish.

2011 tasting - Dark, this Cab has a slightly spicy bouquet followed up with a smooth flavor and a chewy aftertaste.
