2005 Sonoma County Old Vines Zinfandel
St. Francis

Santa Rosa, CA

Aged 15 months in American oak and bottled with a plastic cork.

Price: $21
15.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A dark red, this Zin has a dry, fruity bouquet coupled to a chewy, excellent flavor and a finish that is short but sweet.

2009 tasting - A purple, semi-opaque wine, this Zin has a juicy, intense bouquet followed by an excellent flavor that is chewy, juicy yet dry and has slight buttery hints. The aftertaste is tart and spicy.
