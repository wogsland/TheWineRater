2003 Alexander Valley Estate Carignane
Trentadue Winery

Geyserville, CA

100% Carignane

Harvested on October 15th from vines, half of which are over 100 years old, this Carignane was fermented in steel tanks and then barrel aged 16 months in 90/10 French/American oak barrels.

Price: $16
14.6 % alcohol
750 ml
270 cases produced.

2006 tasting - This Carginane has a purely excellent bouquet and flavor. Definitely worth picking up a bottle.
