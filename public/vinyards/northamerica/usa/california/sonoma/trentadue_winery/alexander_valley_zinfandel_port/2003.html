2003 Alexander Valley Estate Zinfandel Port
Trentadue Winery

Geyserville, CA

100% Zinfandel

This Zinfandel was harvested September 19th and aged in oak barrels twenty months after fermentation.

217 cases produced.

Price: $25
18.6 % alcohol
500 ml

2006 tasting - This Port has a good sweet bouquet & excellent flavor - very dry aftertaste.

2007 tasting - This clearish red Port has a fair bouquet where the brandy overpowers the wine coupled to an intense, complex flavor with an aftertaste that drys the palate.

2010 tasting - An opaque brownish black, this port has an aged, slightly sweet bouquet coupled to a dry, high alcohol, aged flavor and an extremely dry, well-aged aftertaste. It has, however, lost some of the vitality of it's youth.
