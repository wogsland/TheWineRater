2007 Alexander Valley Estate Petite Sirah Port
Trentadue

Geyserville, CA


18.0% alcohol
500 ml

2010 tasting - A completely opaque wine that looks purple around the edges, this port has a solid bouquet with a slightly sweetish flavor and a luxurious aftertaste.

2009 tasting - A black, opaque color with a sweet bouquet among current hints, this port has a chewy, sweet flavor with understated chocolate hints and a very, dry, chewy aftertaste.
