2007 Alexander Valley Merlot Port
Trentadue

Geyserville, CA


Price: $26

2010 tasting - A transparent red, this port has dry, oaky bouquet, a dry, chewy, strongly Merlot flavor and a dry finish.

2009 tasting - An opaque red, this port has a sweet, grapy bouquet coupled to a sweet, slightly chewy flavor that exhibits the nice terroir of the valley. The aftertaste is dry, light and exhibits chocolate hints.
