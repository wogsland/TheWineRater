2005 Sonoma County Zinfandel
Valley of the Moon

Glen Ellen, CA


15.0% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting ($18) - A dark, purply red, this Zin has a fruity, chewy bouquet coupled to a tart, excellent flavor and a chewy, tart finish. Overall the wine is still a bit young though.

2009 tasting ($14) - A dark, opaque wine, this Zin has a chewy, fruity bouquet coupled to an excellent, fruity flavor with a strong grapy finish.
