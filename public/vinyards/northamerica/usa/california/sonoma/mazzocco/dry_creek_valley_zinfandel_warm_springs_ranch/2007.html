2007 Dry Creek Valley Zinfandel Warm Springs Ranch
Mazzocco Sonoma

Healdsburg, CA

Warm Springs Ranch is located in the hills above Dry Creek at 1500.
Aged in French oak barrels 18 months. 500 cases produced.

16.1% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A purplish red, this Zinfandel has a fruity, jammy bouquet coupled to an intense, complex flavor with a chewy aftertaste.

2010 tasting - This transparent red wine has a sweet bouquet coupled to a chewy, round flavor and a tart, grapy, dry finish.
