2006 Alexander Valley Zinfandel Stone
Mazzocco Sonoma

Healdsburg, CA

From the Stone Ranch in Alexander Valley and aged 18 months in French oak barrels before bottling unfined.
600 cases produced.

15.9% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - An excellent Mazzocco Zin.

2009 tasting - A dark, black purple, this Zinfandel has a bouquet with chocolate hints. The flavor is chewy and very fruity with a very dry aftertaste. Still needs age.
