Rancho Zabaco

Healdsburg, CA

www.ranchozabaco.com

Owned by Gallo and named for an old Spanish land grant in the area, they produce mid-level bottlings of Sonoma County. Until the 2006 vintage they also produced the popular Dancing Bull series of California wines, but beginning with that vintage Gallo spun it off to it's own label.

Vintages:

Dancing Bull Chardonnay California 2005
Dancing Bull Merlot California 2004
Dancing Bull Zinfandel California 2001 2004 2005
Dry Creek Valley Reserve Zinfandel Sonoma County 2003
Sonoma Heritage Vines Zinfandel Sonoma County 2006 2008
Chiotti Vineyard Zinfandel Dry Creek Valley 2002
