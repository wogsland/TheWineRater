2005 Dancing Bull Zinfandel California
Rancho Zabaco

Healdsburg, CA


13.9% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting ($9) - A dark transparent red, this Zinfandel has a muted smell coupled to a jammy flavor and a nice, fruity finish. Great with pizza on a hot summer night.

2009 tasting ($10) - A blackish red with a fair bouquet, this red wine has a juicy, unimpressive flavor coupled to a fairly unhinged aftertaste.
