2005 Vintners Blend California Zinfandel
Ravenswood

Sonoma, CA

Mainly from grapes out of Lodi, Sonoma and Mendocino counties.

13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting ($11) - A very dark red, this Zin has a grapy bouquet coupled to a tart, bland flavor and a lackluster aftertaste.

2008 tasting ($9) - A dark transparent red, this Zinfandel has a very fruity bouquet plus a flavor dominated by the Lodi terroir and a chewy, fruity aftertaste.

2009 tasting ($9) - This Zinfandel is a light, transparent red color. The fruity yet stilted bouquet is followed up with an interesting flavor and a tart, numbing finish.
