Tahoe Red
Picchetti Winery

Cupertino, CA

This was a special non-vintage bottling to commemorate the opening of a second tasting room on the Shores of Lake Tahoe in 2007. Mainly a Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot blend, it also contains Cabernet Franc, Lagrein and Petit Verdot.

Price: $29
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This dark purply red blend has a spicu bouquet with floral hints and a spicy, interesting, solid flavor coupled to a tart, jammy aftertaste.

More about Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Lagrein, Merlot, Petit Verdot.
