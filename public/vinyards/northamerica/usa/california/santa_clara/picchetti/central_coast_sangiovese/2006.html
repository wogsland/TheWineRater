2006 Sangiovese Central Coast
Picchetti

Cupertino, CA

Aged in oak (50% French, 40% American & 10% Hungarian).

Price: $31
13.6% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A lighter red, this Sangiovese has a somewhat indescribable bouquet. The flavor, however, is quite sharp at first but mellows to a dry, oaky finish.

2009 tasting - A lighter, transparent red, this Sangiovese has a sweet, mountain bouquet coupled to an excellent, complex flavor and a dry aftertaste with mineral hints.
