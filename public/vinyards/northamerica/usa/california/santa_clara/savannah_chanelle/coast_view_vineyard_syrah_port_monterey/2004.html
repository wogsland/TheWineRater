2007 tasting - This blackish opaque red wine has a a very sweet bouquet which hints of nuts coupled to a sweet, complex flavor and a dry, earthy finish.

2008 tasting - A blackish, opaque red, this port has a deep, fruity bouquet reminiscent of almond flavored christmas cookies. The flavor is perfect with a long, intense, balanced aftertaste.
