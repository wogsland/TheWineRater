NV Batch 88 Starboard
Andrew Quady

Madera, CA

A blend of the traditional Portuguese grarpes Tinta Roriz, Tinta Cao and Tinta Amarella.

Price: $18
20% alcohol
370 ml

2007 tasting - This Port is a nice dark red with quite a bit of sediment. It has a sweet but strangely floral bouquet coupled to a light, complex flavor that is more traditional rather than a California-style port. The aftertaste is biting then sweet.

More about Tinta Roriz, Tinta Amarella, Tinta Cao.
