West Virginia

West Virginia is located in the United States on the western slopes of the Appalachian Mountains. Viticulture is taking hold there in the Kanawha River Valley and other AVAs.

Viticultural Regions:

Kanawha River Valley
Ohio River Valley
Shenandoah Valley
