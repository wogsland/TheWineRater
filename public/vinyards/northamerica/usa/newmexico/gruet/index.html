Gruet Winery

Albuquerque, New Mexico

The Greut Family has long been making Champagne in France and brought their experience to start a winery in New Mexico in 1984. Now their Méthode Champenoise wines are sold thoughout the US. Their vineyards are located near Truth or Consequences, NM (yes, that is really the name of the town), but their tasting room in just off I-25 in Albuquerque.

Tasting Fee: $5 for 5 tastes and a logo glass or flute to take home

www.gruetwinery.com
