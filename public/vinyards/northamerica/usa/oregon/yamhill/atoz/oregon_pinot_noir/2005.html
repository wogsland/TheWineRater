2005 Oregon Pinot Noir

A to Z Wineworks
Newberg, OR

Bottled with a screwcap
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting ($18) - This dark red Pinot Noir has a light bouquet coupled to an excellent, fruity flavor and a long but light aftertaste.

2008 tasting ($20) - A dark, transparent red, this Pinot has a light bouquet coupled to a juicy, complex flavor that is perhaps a bit young. The finish is slightly floral and the wine improves as the bottle is open to the air.
