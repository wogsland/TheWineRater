2011 tasting - This nearly clear wine has a grassy, tart bouquet and a tart, sweet, slightly frizzante flavor coupled to a tart finish.

2010 tasting - This clear white has a muted bouquet coupled to a light, sweetish flavor and a chewy aftertaste.

2009 tasting - A clear white, this Müller Thurgau has a grassy, intense bouquet coupled to a grassy flavor and a green, light finish.
