2007 Olalla Vineyard Umpqua Valley Pinot Noir
Torii Mor

Dundee, OR

4.5 acres planted in 1973 with 115 and Pommard clones. This wine is a blend of 80% Pommard and 20% 115 aged in French oak. 252 cases produced

Price: $28
13.3% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A light, transparent red, this Pinot has a muted, slightly sweet bouquet. The flavor which follows is light with an excellent body and slight spicy finish hinting of currants.
