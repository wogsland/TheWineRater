2007 Chehalem Mountains Select Pinot Noir
Torii Mor Winery

Dundee, OR

46.15% Hawks Vineyard (114 & Hanzell clones), 30.77% Alloro Vineyard (Pommard clone) and 23.08% Arbre Vert Vineyard (Pommard clone) aged in French oak.

Price: $28
13.6% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - This clear, red wine has a slightly brownish tinge. The bouquet in pure Oregon and the flavor which follows is light and yet surprisingly complex. It is tart with hints of fruit and a long finish.
