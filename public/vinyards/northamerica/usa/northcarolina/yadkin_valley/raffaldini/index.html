Raffaldini

Ronda, NC

The Italian family of Raffaldini established their New World vineyards in the Swan Creek area of the Yadkin Valley. They grow a variety of Italian and French grapes for vinification and were instrumental in the creation of the Swan Creek AVA in 2008. Raffaldini has been a leader in exploring the possiblity of the Valley's terroir and improving the quality of the wines made there. No doubt they will provide us with pleasant surprises for years to come.
