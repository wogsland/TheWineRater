2005 Chardonnay Yadkin Valley
Elkin Creek Vineyard

Elkin, NC

Aged in American oak.

Price: $18
12.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This light yellow Chardonnay has a grassy, oaky bouquet paired to a light, buttery taste and a buttery, oaky finish. One of the best Chardonnays in the Yadkin Valley - world class!

2008 tasting - This clear yellow Chardonnay has a strong, grassy bouquet coupled to an excellent, mellow flavor that is complex exhibiting grassy and buttery hints. These continue into a finish soft as velvet.
