2004 Yadkin Valley Nebbiolo
Round Peak

Mt. Airy, NC

This Nebbiolo spends one year in the barrel.

Price: $20
12% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This red, transparent wine has a light bouquet and a tart, complex flavor with a very dry aftertaste.

2008 tasting - A clear red, this Nebbiolo has a strong, tannic character to its bouquet accompanied by sweet notes. The flavor has a tinge of East Coast varietal to it which improves with oxidation and a fair, long, Eastern finish.
