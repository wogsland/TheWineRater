Round Peak Vineyards
Mt. Airy, NC


roundpeak.com

Vinifying only grapes grown in the Yadkin Valley AVA, Round Peak was started by two Davidson Alumni, George Little and Lee Martin, in 2000.

Vintages:

Yadkin Valley Chardonnay 2005
Yadkin Valley Nebbiolo 2004
Yadkin Valley Sangiovese 2006
Yadkin Valley Reserve Sangiovese 2006
Yadkin Valley Cabernet Sauvignon 2004
