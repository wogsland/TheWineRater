2005 Red Barn Blend Yadkin Valley
Grassy Creek

Elkin, NC

A blend of 60% Merlot, 30% Syrah and 10% Sangiovese.

Price: $16
750 ml
12.4% alcohol

2007 tasting - This light red wine has a flowery bouquet and a very floral, smooth flavor with a dry aftertaste. Probably the best red I've tasted from the Yadkin Valley.

2008 tasting - A clear red, the dry, fruity bouquet precedes a very dry, tart, solid flavor and a long, dry aftertaste.
