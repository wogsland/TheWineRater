2004 Yadkin Valley Port Estate Bottled
Shelton Vineyards

Dobson, NC

Made from mostly Cabernet Franc.

Price: $16
19% alcohol
375 ml

2007 tasting - This dark red Port has a sweet bouquet and flavor with grapy hints and a sweet aftertaste.

2008 tasting - This blackish red port has nice, thin legs. A very light smell with sweet hints is followed up by a sweet, grapy, eastern flavor with a grapy, slightly sweet finish.

2009 tasting - A dark red, this port has a sweet, eastern bouquet coupled to a tangy, sweet flavor and a tangy, very eastern aftertaste.
