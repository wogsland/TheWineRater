Love My Goat Red
Bully Hill Vineyards

Hammondsport, NY

Price: $8
750 ml
11% alcohol

2007 tasting - This brownish red wine starts off very yeasty when first opened but the bouquet eventually mellows to a jammy, peppery one. The flavor is jammy and very sweet with a sweet aftertaste hinting of chemicals.
