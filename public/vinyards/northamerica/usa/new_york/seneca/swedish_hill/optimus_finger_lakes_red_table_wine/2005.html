2005 Optimus Finger Lakes Red Table Wine
Swedish Hill

A blend of Cabernet Franc 53%, Cabernet Sauvignon 39% and Merlot 8%.
466 cases produced.

Price: $14
13.0% alcohol

2009 tasting - A dark, transparent red, this wine has a chewy, young bouquet coupled to a flavor that is dry while still being chewy and fruity. The aftertaste is extremely dry with slight eastern hints.
