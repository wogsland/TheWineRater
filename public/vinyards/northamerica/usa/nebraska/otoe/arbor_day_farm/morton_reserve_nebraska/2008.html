2010 tasting - A light color with a sweet bouquet, this wine has a sweet, slightly tart flavor coupled to a yummy aftertaste!

2009 tasting - A clear white, this blend has a sweet, grapy bouquet. The flavor is sweet with apple hints and a leafy aftertaste.
