James Arthur Vineyards

Raymond, NE

www.jamesarthurvineyards.com

Growing only hybrid grapes, this winery is located just north of Nebraska's capitol, Lincoln. They have 20 acres onsite, 20 acres at another location and source some of their grapes from other growers.

Tasting Fee: $4-5 (includes glass)

Vintages:

Heartland White 2007
Traminette White American Table Wine 2007
Vignoles 2007
San Realto 2007
White St. Croix 2007
DeChaunac 2005
Frontenac 2006
Game Bird Red 2006
