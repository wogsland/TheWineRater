Davenport Red
Stonehaus Winery

Crossville, TN

Made from the Concord grape.

Price: $10
750 ml

2007 tasting - This light ruby red has a muted bouquet with a strong Concord flavor and a dry finish.

2008 tasting - This very light red has a strong and sweet Concord bouquet coupled to a juicy flavor and a light Concord finish. This wine tastes so much like grapejuice one almost wonders if it's fermented!
