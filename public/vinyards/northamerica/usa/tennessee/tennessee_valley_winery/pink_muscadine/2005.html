2005 Tennessee Pink Muscadine
Tennessee Valley Winery

Loudon, TN

A blend of white and red Muscadines.

Price: $12
750 ml

2007 tasting - This pink wine has a muscadine bouquet coupled to a sweet, interesting flavor and a long muscadine aftertaste.

2008 tasting - The color of this wine lives up to its name. It has a sweet, grapy bouquet and a flavor wherein sweetness overpowers complexity but the grapiness strongly reappears in the finish.
