Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania is one of the original 13 United States. The Central Delaware Valley, Cumberland Valley and Lake Erie AVAs cross the borders into several other states. There are also viticultural areas located entirely within Pennsylvania.

Central Delaware Valley
Chester County
Cumberland Valley
Lake Erie
