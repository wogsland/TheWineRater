2006 Claret Monticello
Veritas

Afton, VA

A blend of 90% Cabernet Franc and 10% Petit Verdot aged 9 months in oak.

Price: $16
750 ml

2008 tasting - This dark red has a slightly sweet, slightly floral bouquet coupled to a sweetish flavor wherein the Petit Verdot mutes and sweetens the Cabernet Franc. The finish is light and dry.

2009 tasting - This purplish red has a spicy, eastern bouquet coupled to a light, fruity, very eastern flavor and a slightly dry aftertaste.
