Mountain Rose Virginia

Afton Mountain Vineyards
Afton, VA

A non-vintage blend of mostly Cabernet Sauvignon and Cabernet Franc with smaller amounts of Merlot, Sangiovese and Pinot Noir.

Price: $12
750 ml

2008 tasting - A very light red, this blend has a very sweet, grapy bouquet coupled to a grapy, sweet, juicy flavor and a sweet, fair aftertaste.
