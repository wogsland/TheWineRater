Barboursville Vineyards

Barboursville, VA

Founded by Italians in 1976, Barboursville Vineyards was the first in Virginia to focus exclusively on European grape varietals. Their huge tasting room is often abuzz with visitors and $4 tasting fee buys a glass which can be re-used anytime one returns.
