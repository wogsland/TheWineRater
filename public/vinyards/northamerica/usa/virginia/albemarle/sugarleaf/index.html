Sugarleaf Vineyards

North Garden, VA

www.sugarleafvineyards.com

Sugarleaf is a very small production vineyard located on a hillside in the Appalachian foothills. Jerry and Lauren Bias are the proprietors, opening the winery in 2007.

Vintages:

Chardonnay Monticello 2006
Vidal Blanc Monticello 2006
Viognier Monticello 2006
Cabernet Franc Monticello 2006
Cabernet Sauvignon Monticello 2006
Petit Verdot Monticello 2006
