Cru Aperitif Wine Mélange Nouveau

Kluge Estate Winery and Vineyard
Charlottesville, VA

This non-vintage wine is made from Chardonnay juice fortified with Chardonnay brandy and aged in Whiskey barrels.

Price: $28
18.0% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - This yellow wine has a very sweet, fruity bouquet coupled to a strong, sweet flavor and a nice brandy finish.

2009 tasting - A light yellow, this fortified wine has a young, very grapy bouquet coupled to a sweet, orangy flavor and a long, flowery aftertaste.
