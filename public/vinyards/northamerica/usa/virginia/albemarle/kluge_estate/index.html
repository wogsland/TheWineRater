Kluge Estate
Winery and Vineyard

Charlottesville, VA

www.klugeestateonline.com

The Kluge Estate Farm Shop serves as a tasting room and restaurant. While the light fare is rather pricy (~$15 a plate), it makes a worthy companion to the tasting experience. In addition to still wines they also produce a variety of sparklers by the Methode Champenoise developed in the French region of Champagne.
