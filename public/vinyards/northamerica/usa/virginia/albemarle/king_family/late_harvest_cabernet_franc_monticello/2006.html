2006 Late Harvest Cabernet Franc Monticello

King Family Vineyards
Crozet, VA

Made in the style of an Italian Ripasso, the grapes were picked in October and then dried for six weeks before fermentation to concentrate the sugars.

Harvest sugar 41 Brix, residual sugar 8%

110 cases produced.

Price: $25
15.1% alcohol
375 ml

2008 tasting - This dark, slightly brownish red wine has a sweet, interesting bouquet coupled to a sweet, complex flavor and a long, dry finish which becomes sweet only at the very end.

2009 tasting - An excellent, deep, fruity bouquet coupled to a dark, opaque red color and a light complex flavor that could have been made in Italy. The finish is dry and chewy.
