2005 Cabernet Franc Virginia

Lake Anna Winery
Spotsylvania, VA

78% Cabernet Franc blended with 13% Petit Verdot and 9% Merlot.

Price: $16
750 ml

2008 tasting - This light red wine has a fruity bouquet with an equally fruity flavor that is also light and dry. This dryness continues into the finish along with slightly tannic hints.

2009 tasting - This transparent red has a sweet, eastern bouquet paired with an eastern flavor and a dry aftertaste.
