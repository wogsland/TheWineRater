Parnell, Auckland, NZ

750 ml

2008 tasting - A nearly clear white, this Sauvignon Blanc has a strong, fruity bouquet coupled to a tart, intense taste and a complex, long, sour apple finish.

2009 tasting - This clear, white wine has long legs and a grassy bouquet. The flavor is light and slightly sweet with a finish that is grassy then tart.
