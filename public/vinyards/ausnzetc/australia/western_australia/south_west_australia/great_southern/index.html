Great Southern

Western Australia

The Great Southern region of the South West Australia zone is located in Western Australia. It was declared a GI on 30 October 1996 and has a variety of sub-regions.

Sub-Regions:

Albany
Denmark
Frankland River
Mount Barker
Porongurup
