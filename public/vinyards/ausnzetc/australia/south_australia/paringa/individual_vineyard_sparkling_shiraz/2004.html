2004 Individual Vineyard Sparkling Shiraz South Australia
Paringa

Clarendon, SA


Price: $11
12.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting ($11) - This is a completely opaque dark purple wine. This Shiraz has a light and fruity bouquet paired to a very sweet and bubbly flavor which reminds me of the Santa Cruz Mtns. terrior. The aftertaste is quite drying while hinting of sweetness. Best served cold.

2008 tasting ($11) - An opaque purple, this fizzy wine has a deep bouquet coupled to a fizzy, sweet flavor and a fruity, intense aftertaste.

2009 tasting ($9) - An opaque red, this wine has a sweetish bouquet coupled to a fizzy, young flavor and a mountain finish.

2010 tasting ($9) - An opaque, sparkling, deep red, this Shiraz has a doughy, CO2 bouquet coupled to a fizzy, chewy flavor with great mouth feel. Seems to be a bit past it's prime though.
