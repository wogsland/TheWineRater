2004 McLaren Vale d'Arry's Original Shiraz Grenache
d'Arenberg

McLaren Vale, SA

50% Shiraz and 50% Grenache.
Foot-trod, basket-pressed, aged 18 months in oak and bottled with a screwcap.

Price: $21
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A cloudy red, this wine has a fruity, spicy bouquet coupled to a complex, jammy, excellent flavor. This is followed up by an intensely dry aftertaste which is also quite chewy.
