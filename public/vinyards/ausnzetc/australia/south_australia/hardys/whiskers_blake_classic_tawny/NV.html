NV Whiskers Blake Classic Tawny
Hardy's

Reynella, SA


Price: $15
18% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This light reddish brown wine has a solid bouquet hinting of nuts coupled to a light, understated flavor and an extremely nutty longlasting finish.

2008 tasting - This brown port has a sweet and inviting smell followed by a sweet, complex flavor and a classic Aussie finish that is nutty and sweet.
