2007 Chardonnay Eden Valley Australia Estates
Greg Norman

Nuriootpa, SA


Price: $15
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - A green-tinted yellow, this Chardonnay has a mellowing bouquet which transports the senses to a sunny afternoon in the hills. The flavor which follows is deep and intense. The grassy, toffee and butter hints are underlain by a tartness that bites in the finish. This will probably soften with age, but why bother when it's excellent now!
