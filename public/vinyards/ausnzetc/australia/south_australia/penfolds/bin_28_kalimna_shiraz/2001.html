2001 Bin 28 Kalimna Shiraz South Australia
Penfolds

Magill, South Australia

Aged 12 months in American oak.

Price: $15
14.2% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - This Shiraz has a nice, purply hue and an extremely fruity bouquet coupled to a chewy flavor. Surprisingly undry, this wine has aged very well and will no doubt continue to develop in the bottle for years to come. Decant to remove the sediment before drinking.

2010 tasting - An opaque red with some sediment, this Shiraz has a fruity yet aged bouquet coupled to well-aged flavor and a dry finish. With a bit of aeration the fruitiness really opens up.
