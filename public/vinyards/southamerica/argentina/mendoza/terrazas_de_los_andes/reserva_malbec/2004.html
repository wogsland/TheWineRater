2004 Reserva Malbec
Terrazas de los Andes

Mendoza, Argentina

Estate grown at a 3500 foot elevation. Aged in new oak (90% French and 10% American).

Price: $17
13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2007 tasting - This dark red wine has a an interesting buttery, oaky bouquet to introduce the wine. This is followed up by an excellent flavor which hints of buttery popcorn and a long complex aftertaste. Definitely worth picking up a bottle.
