2006 Reserva Privada Cabernet Sauvignon Syrah Maipo Valley
Casillero del Diablo

Chile

The 2nd vintage by Concha y Toro made from 85% Cabernet Sauvignon of the Pirque vineyard and 15% Syrah of the Peumo vineyards. Aged in French oak for 14 months.

Price: $16
14.0% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - This blackish red wine has a rich, berry bouquet coupled to a biting, puckered flavor and an extremely dry, somewhat chewy aftertaste. It needs time to open up, but it will be excellent one day.
