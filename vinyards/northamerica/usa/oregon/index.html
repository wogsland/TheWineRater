Oregon

Oregon is located on the Pacific coast of the United States north of California and contains all the climactic variation of its southern neighbor. Not surprisingly, Oregon produces some great wines - especially in the fertile Willamette Valley. Several of the state's AVAs straddle its border with Washington.
