Grassy Creek Vineyard & Winery

Elkin, NC

www.grassycreekvineyard.com

Built on the property of an old hunting lodge and a dairy, Grassy Creek is a place with copius history. As such, they use the Klondike Farms name to label sweet wines, which they sell in old milk bottles.

Grassy Creek Vintages:

Chardonnay Yadkin Valley 2005
Red Barn Blend Yadkin Valley 2005
Merlot North Carolina 2005

Klondike Farms Vintages:

Sweet Guernsey Red Yadkin Valley 2005
