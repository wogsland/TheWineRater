NV Pino Vino Red Table Wine Yadkin Valley
Raffaldini Vineyards

Ronda, NC

This wine contains everything but the kitchen sink: Dolcetto, Malvasia Nera, Nebbiolo, Syrah, Primitivo, Sangiovese, Barbera, Montepulciano, Petit Verdot and Merlot. All profits also go to charity in honor of the dog on the label.

750 ml

2010 tasting - This light red blend has a very eastern bouquet coupled to a spicy flavor with strong terroir and a dry, interesting finish.

More about Barbera, Dolcetto, Malvasia Nera, Merlot, Montepulciano, Nebbiolo, Petit Verdot, Primitivo, Sangiovese, Syrah.
