Cellar 4201

East Bend, NC


The winery takes its name from its origins in a basement at 4201 Apperson Rd. in East Bend. They only vinify grapes from the Yadkin Valley.

Vintages:

Sangiovese Yadkin Valley North Carolina 2005
Merlot Yadkin Valley North Carolina 2005
