Nobos

Plymouth, CA


Nobos is Sobon backwards, as they are the family which produces these wines at their Shenandoah Vineyards facility. For these wines they source the grapes from outside Amador County.

Vintages:

Chardonnay Monterey 2007
Cabernet Sauvignon Dry Creek Valley 2005
Merlot Dry Creek Valley 2005
