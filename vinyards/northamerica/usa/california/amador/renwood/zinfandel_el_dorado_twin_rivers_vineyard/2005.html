2011 tasting - A transparent red, this wine has a deep, spicy bouquet with woodland notes – cedar, perhaps? The flavor is light, spicy and very smooth with a dry, long-lasting aftertaste evocative of the terroir.

2009 tasting - This light red wine has a chewy, spicy flavor coupled to a long, sweet finish exhibiting pepper hints.
