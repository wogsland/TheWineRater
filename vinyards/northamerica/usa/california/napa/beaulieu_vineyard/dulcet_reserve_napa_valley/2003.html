2003 Dulcet Reserve Napa Valley
Beaulieu Vineyard

Rutherford, CA

A blend of 76% Cabernet Sauvignon and 24% Syrah.

Price: $40
750 ml
14.4% alcohol

2007 tasting - A dark blackish red, it has a very floral, complex bouquet and an interesting, sweet taste coupled to a long, complex finish.

2008 tasting - A dark, purply red, this wine has an excellent, dark, jammy bouquet coupled to a tart, complex flavor with hints of raisins. Very dry and very smooth the wine has a dry, long aftertaste.
