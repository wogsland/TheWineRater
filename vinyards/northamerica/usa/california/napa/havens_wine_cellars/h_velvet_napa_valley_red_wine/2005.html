2005 H Velvet Napa Valley Red Wine
Havens

Napa, CA

A blend of 60% Cabernet Sauvignon with 40% Syrah.

14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - This dark, red wine has an interesting, complex bouquet: currants, berries, age & hint of oak. The flavor which follows is nicely balanced and has a slightly spicy aftertaste.

2011 tasting - An opqaue red with an oaky bouquet, this wine has a sweet, chewy flavor coupled to a slighty spicy aftertaste.

2010 tasting - This black, opaque wine has a strong, Syrah-dominated bouquet coupled to an excellently blended flavor and a dry, grapy finish.
