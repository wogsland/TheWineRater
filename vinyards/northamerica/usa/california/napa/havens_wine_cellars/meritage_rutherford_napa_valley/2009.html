2009 Meritage Rutherford Napa Valley
Havens

Napa, CA


Price: $20
14.3% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - Definitely not of the same quality as when Michael Havens ran the place. There are a lot of flavors in this blend all happening at once which don't really go together. If it didn't say Napa Valley on the bottle I'd peg it as a wine made from grapes from the Central Valley.
