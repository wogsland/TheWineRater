1999 Napa Valley Zinfandel
Ballentine Vineyards

St. Helena, Napa County, CA

Price: $25
14.9% alcohol
750 ml

2006 tasting ($25) - Excellent bouquet hints of chocolate. Tastes like a good California wine with a nice dry finish.

2009 tasting ($17) - Having another go at the first Zinfandel I ever tried . . . This semi-opaque red has a fruity, oaky bouquet. The flavor is a wave of complexity. It is dry yet sweet, fruity yet tart and finishes with hints of pepper. Seems a bit unhinged though.
