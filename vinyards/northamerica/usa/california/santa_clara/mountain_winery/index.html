The Mountain Winery

Saratoga, CA

www.mountainwinery.com

Originally called Paul Masson Mountain Winery they have operated continuously since 1852 (including during Prohibition). They have an arena with a summer concert series.

$7 tasting fee includes wineglass.

Vintages:

Chardonnay NV
White Zinfandel NV
Chardonnay Edna Valley 2005
Merlot Napa Valley Howell Mountain 2002
Syrah Lodi 2004
Cabernet Sauvignon Napa Valley 2003
