2006 Dry Creek Valley Zinfandel Pony
Mazzocco

Healdsburg, CA

Winemaker Antoine Favero aged this single vineyard wine in French oak for 18 months and bottled unfined.
500 cases produced.

Price: $32
16.1% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A dark red, this Zinfandel has a jammy, young bouquet with a strong exhibition of the Dry Creek Valley terroir. The young flavor is excellent with a tart, short finish.

2009 tasting - A semi-transparent red, this Dry Creek Zin has a fruity bouquet accompanied by a dry, chewy flavor with prune hints and an extremely chewy aftertaste. I don't think it's peaked yet.
