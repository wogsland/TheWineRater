2007 Dry Creek Valley Zinfandel West Dry Creek Reserve
Mazzocco Sonoma

Healdsburg, CA

Aged in French oak for 18 months and bottled unfined.
550 cases made.

15.7% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - A dark, transparent red, this Zanfandel has a dry bouquet with nice terroir and an excellent flavor with a dry, sweet, slightly chewy aftertaste.

2011 tasting - A dark red with long legs, this Zin has an EXTREMELY fruity bouquet. The equally strong flavor is evocative of the Dry Creek Valley terroir with a very chewy finish.

2010 tasting - A light, purplish red, this Zin has a nice, woody bouquet coupled to a chewy, complicated flavor with slight butter hints in the finish.
