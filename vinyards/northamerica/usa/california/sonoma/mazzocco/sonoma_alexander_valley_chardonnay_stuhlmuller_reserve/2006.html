2006 Sonoma Alexander Valley Chardonnay Stuhlmuller Reserve
Mazzocco

Healdsburg, CA

Winemaker Antoine Favero aged 22 months in French oak and bottled unfined.
800 cases produced.

Price: $36
14.4% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A golden yellow Chardonnay which exhibits oakiness, youth and apple hints on the bouquet, this wine is overpoweringly tart with a sweet finish amongst buttercream hints. It definitely needs more age to mellow.

2009 tasting - This golden yellow Chardonnay has a dry, grassy bouquet coupled to a moist, buttery flavor. The aftertaste is tart, buttery and long.
