2008 Dry Creek Valley Zinfandel Smith Orchard Reserve
Mazzocco Sonoma

Sonoma, CA

550 cases aged 18 months in French oak.

14.9% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - A muted, transparent red, this Zinfandel has nice legs. The jammy, spicy bouquet is followed by a young, thin flavor which is not strongly bound to the terroir. The aftertaste starts out spicy and evolves into jammy.

2011 tasting - A dark purple with an oaky, young bouquet, this Zinfandel was the first wine I tasted in 2011. The flavor is chewy, round and slighty green with a spicy aftertaste. Clearly it needs to spend some more time in the bottle.
