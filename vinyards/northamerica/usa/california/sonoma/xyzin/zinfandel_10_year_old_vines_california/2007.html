From the Vine Aged Series.

Price: $16

2009 tasting - A dark red with fat legs and a tart bouquet, this Zinfandel has a tart, dry flavor that sends the tastebuds all over followed by a short, spicy aftertaste. At times chewy and at others peppery; sometimes jammy, others dry - this wine exemplifies most of the characteriships which make California Zinfandel interesting.
