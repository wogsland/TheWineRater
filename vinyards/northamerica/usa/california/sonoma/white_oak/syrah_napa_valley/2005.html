2005 Syrah Napa Valley
White Oak Vineyards & Winery

Healdsburg, CA

100% Syrah, manually harvested, fermented in stainless steel with Pasteur Red yeast for 8 days, held on the skins a further 12 days, racked into oak barrels (6% American, 74% French and 20% Hungarian) and bottled after 18 months.1941 cases produced.

Price: $26
14.6% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - An opaque red, this Syrah has an oaky bouquet coupled to a light flavor with strong berry notes and a spicy finish.
