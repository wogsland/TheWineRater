2007 Reserve Zinfandel Sonoma County
Trader Joe's

Healdsburg, CA

Vinted and bottled by Mazzocco Sonoma.

Price: $10
15.2% alcohol
750 ml

2012 tasting - A transparent red with a jammy and awesome bouquet, this Zin has a jammy, fair flavor and a spicyish aftertaste.

2011 tasting - A semi-transparent red, this Zinfandel has a strong, juicy bouquet coupled to a spicy, intense flavor which lasts a nice, long time on the palate.

2010 tasting - A chewy Sonoma flavor with a dry bouquet and aftertaste. Solid and drinkable with enough alcohol for good ageability.

2009 tasting - An excellent solid red, this Zin has an intense, spicy flavor coupled to a dry, slightly chewy finish.
