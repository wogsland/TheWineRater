1994 Allègre Russian River Valley Rhône Style Red Wine
Philip Staley

Healdsburg, CA

100% Grenache from the Staley Vineyard.

Price: $12
13.9% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A slightly cloudy, light red Grenache, this wine has a bouquet that is mainly alcohol with some fruit. The aged flavor is very dry with a tart, chewy aftertaste.

2009 tasting - A dark red, this wine has a muted bouquet coupled to a tart flavor that finishes in sort of a soft dryness. It has aged well and probably could have gone longer.
