2006 Merlot Feingold Vineyard Sonoma Mountain
Benziger

Glen Ellen, CA

Made from organically grown grapes. Certified Organic by Stellar.

Price: $14
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A dark, transparent red with a brown tinge, this Merlot has a deep, oaky bouquet in which one can smell the alcohol. The strong Merlot flavor has berry hints and a dry, tart aftertaste.

2009 tasting - This dark red Merlot has a dry bouquet with fruit hints. The flavor is light and dry while also being chewy and fruity. The finish is also dry.
