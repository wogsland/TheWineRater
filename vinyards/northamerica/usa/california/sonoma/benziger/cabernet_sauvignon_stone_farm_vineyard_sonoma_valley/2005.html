2005 Cabernet Sauvignon Stone Farm Vineyard Sonoma Valley
Benziger Family Winery

Glen Ellen, CA

Organically grown on loam soil, these grapes were harvested on Oct 19th, 2005, aged 17 months in French oak after vinification and bottled May 19th, 2007. 32 barrels (750 cases) produced.

Certified organic by Stellar Certification Services.

Price: $20
15.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A dark, intense red, this wine has a fruity, young bouquet coupled to a complex, chewy flavor with chocolate and berry hints and a chewy finish.
