2009 Gypsy California White Wine Blend
Chariot

Healdsburg, CA

Vinted and bottled by Jim Neal Wines.

13.0% alcohol
750 ml

2011 tasting - A light yellow color, this blend has a strong fruity bouquet (Viognier perhaps?). The flavor is sweet and fruity with a moist finish. Overall, the alcohol content is a bit high.
