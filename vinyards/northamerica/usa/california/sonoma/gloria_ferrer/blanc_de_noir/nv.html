Blanc de Noirs Sonoma County Sparkling Wine
Gloria Ferrer

Sonoma, CA

A non-vintage blend of Pinot Noir and Chardonnay fermented in bottle by the méthode Champenoise.

Price: $19
12.9% alcohol
750 ml

2006 tasting (92% Pinot Noir, 8% Chardonnay) - This Blanc de Noir has a good smell and a good tart taste.

2009 tasting - A pink sparkler, this Blanc de Noirs has an apple, tart bouquet. The flavor is fizzy and light with an extremely tart finish.
