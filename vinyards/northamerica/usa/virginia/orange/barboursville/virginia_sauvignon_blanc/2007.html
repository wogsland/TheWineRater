2007 Virginia Sauvignon Blanc
Barboursville Vineyards

Barboursville, VA

Blended with a bit of Semillon.

Price: $13
12.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A nearly clear wine, this Sauvignon Blanc has a light bouquet coupled to a tart, citrusy flavor and a tart aftertaste with Granny Smith apple hints.

2009 tasting - This light yellow wine has an excellent, tart bouquet accompanied by a tart, nice, young flavor with apple hints.

2010 tasting - A clear white with a cardboard bouquet, this Sauvignon Blanc has a light flavor with grassy hints and a tart finish.
