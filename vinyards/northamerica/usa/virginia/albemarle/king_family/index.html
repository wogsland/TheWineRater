King Family Vineyards

Crozet, VA

www.kingfamilyvineyards.com

The King Family started out growing grapes but eventually moved into vinification as well. They currently have 16 acres growing 5 different grape varietals. Typically they drop about half the fruit from the vines and were some of the first in the Monticello AVA to emphasize quality over quantity in grape production.
