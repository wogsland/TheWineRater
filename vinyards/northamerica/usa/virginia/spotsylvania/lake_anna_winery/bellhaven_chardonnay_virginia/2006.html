2006 Bellhaven Chardonnay Virginia

Lake Anna Winery
Spotsylvania, VA

96% Chardonnay blended with 4% Viognier.
Fermented and aged 6 months in stainless steel tanks.

Price: $14
750 ml

2008 tasting - A light yellow color, this Chardonnay has an oaky, grassy bouquet coupled to a nice, oaky flavor and a nice, slightly buttery finish.

2009 tasting - A light yellow, grassy bouqueted wine, this Chardonnay has a light, eastern flavor and a short, sweet aftertaste.
