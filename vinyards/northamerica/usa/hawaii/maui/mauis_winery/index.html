Maui's Winery

Ulupalakua, HI

Maui's Winery owns the small Tedeschi Vineyard on the slopes of the Haleakala volcano, which unfortunately doesn't provide very many grapes. Ergo they import most of their grapes from California. They produce non-vintage wines, only one of which, Ulupakakua Red, sometimes contains any of the Tedeschi grapes. They also ferment pineapple juice in several ways, the sparkling variety of which is rather interesting.

www.mauiwine.com
