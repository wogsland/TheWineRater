NV Messis Port
Rancho Ponte Vineyard

Fredricksburg, TX

A non-vintage blend of Roussanne and Cortese grapes from near Brownsville.

Price: $37

2011 tasting - This light orange colored wine has a bouquet with hints of muscat and sherry. The flavor which follows is very much like a sherry and has a strong aftertaste that's slightly sweet.
