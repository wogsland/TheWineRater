1991 Estate Bottled Hermann Port
Stone Hill Winery

Hermann, Missouri

Aged in oak casks.

Price: $40
19.5% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - The cork disintegrated with the corkscrew, indicating a well-aged bottle (bought directly from the winery). An opaque, blackish red, this port has a deep, sweet bouquet coupled to a fruity flavor that is still incredibly young! The sediment requires decanting. The aftertaste is intense, fruity and eastern.
