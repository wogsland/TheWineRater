Cream Sherry

Stone Hill Winery

Hermann, MO

This is Stone Hill's blended, non-vintage, Sherry-style fortified wine.

500 ml

2006 tasting ($15, 18.5% Alcohol) - This Sherry has a strong alcohol bouquet and a very good flavor.

2008 tasting ($16, 18.5% Alcohol) - Golden brown in color, this wine has a dry, excellent bouquet. The taste is equally excellent; sunny with nutty, chewy and thick attributes which last well into the long finish.

2009 tasting ($16, 19% alcohol, Missouri) - A dark yellow, this Sherry has a strong, excellent bouquet coupled to an interesting complex flavor and a nutty aftertaste. Quickly becoming one of my favorite North American Sherries.

2010 tasting - (19% alcohol, Missouri) Aged in small oak barrels. A light , golden brown with a strong bouquet hinting of nuts & oranges, this wine has a sweet bouquet with a nutty finish.
