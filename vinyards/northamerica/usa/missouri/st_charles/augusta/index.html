Augusta

Missouri

The Augusta AVA, located in Missouri, was the first AVA declared in the US on June 20, 1980. The appellation is home to vineyards of the state's growing winegrowing industry. Geographically it is situated on the northern bank of the Missouri River in St. Charles County.
