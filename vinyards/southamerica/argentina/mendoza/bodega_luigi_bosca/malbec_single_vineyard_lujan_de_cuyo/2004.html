2004 Malbec Single Vineyard Luján de Cuyo
Luigi Bosca

Argentina

This wine is made from 70 year old vines at 960m altitude and was aged 14 months in oak.

Price: $20
14.5% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - This dark purplish red wine has a dry, oaky bouquet which is paired to a flavor that is complex, interesting, fruity and intense! As the wine passes the palate the flavors are tart and fruity, eventually becoming strangely buttery in the aftertaste. This Malbec is interesting indeed.
