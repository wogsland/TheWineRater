2005 Gran Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon Valle del Maule
Candelaria

Chile

Produced and bottled by Via Wines Group. The grapes are hand harvested from the Chilcas Vineyard in the northern part of the Maule Valley. The resultant wine is aged in a mixture of French and American oak barrels for 12 months before bottling.

Price: $14
13.8% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - An opaque red, this wine has a buttery bouquet followed up by a tart, intense flavor and a dry finish.
