2006 Voluptuous Beauty
Valle Central, Chile
Cabernet Franc Carmenère
Oops

A blend of 84% Cabernet Franc and 16% Carmenère.

Price: $10
13.9% alcohol
750 ml

2008 tasting - A opaque red wine, it looks almost black in the glass. The blend has a dry, oaky bouquet coupled to a tart, curt, slightly vegetal flavor and an exquisitely dry, complex finish. It's a solid wine for the money.
