2006 Misterio de Uclés
M

Cuenca, España

A blend of 85% Tempranillo and 15% Syrah aged 3 months in American oak and bottled with a plastic cork.

Bottled by Bodegas Fontana.

Price: $10
14.0% alcohol
750 ml

2009 tasting - A dark, purply red, this wine has an interesting, fruity bouquet coupled to a dry, chewy, fruity flavor. The chewiness gets stronger in the aftertaste and is complimented with butter hints.

More about Syrah, Tempranillo.
