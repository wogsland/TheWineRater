2006 Koonunga Hill Shiraz Cabernet South Australia
Penfolds

Magill, South Australia

Bottled with a plastic cork.
A blend of 78% Shiraz and 22% Cabernet Sauvignon.

13.5% alcohol
750 ml

2010 tasting ($9) - A dark purple-opaque wine cloudy with sediment, this blend has a fruity bouquet with a sharp hint. The flavor is dry, smooth and easy-drinking with a tart, very dry aftertaste.

2008 tasting ($11) - A dark purple, opaque wine, this blend has a dry, yeasty bouquet coupled to a tart, fair flavor and a short aftertaste.
